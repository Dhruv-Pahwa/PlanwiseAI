La Tomatina Itinerary

Overview

Experience the world's biggest food fight in Buñol, Spain, where thousands of people gather to throw tomatoes in a fun and vibrant festival.

Day-wise Plan

Day 1: Arrival in Valencia & explore the city.
Day 2: Travel to Buñol, participate in La Tomatina & enjoy after-party events.
Day 3: Visit Albufera Natural Park & relax at Valencia’s beaches.

Budget Estimate

Budget range: ₹25,000 - ₹70,000 (including accommodation, food, transport, and festival entry)

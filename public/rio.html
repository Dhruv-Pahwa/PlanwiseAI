Rio Carnival Itinerary

Overview

Experience the world's biggest carnival in Brazil, featuring extravagant parades, samba performances, and vibrant street parties.

Day-wise Plan

Day 1: Arrive in Rio & explore Copacabana Beach.
Day 2: Witness the Samba Parade at the Sambadrome.
Day 3: Join street parties & visit Christ the Redeemer.

Budget Estimate

Budget range: ₹80,000 - ₹2,00,000 (including flights, accommodation, food, and event tickets)

Best Time to Visit
